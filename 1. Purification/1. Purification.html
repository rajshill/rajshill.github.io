1.1 Dhauti

1.1.1 Antara Dhauti

1.1.1.1 Vatasara Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Sit Normally in any Asana.
  • Neck & Spine Straight.
  • Make Kaki Mudra (Crow beak).
  • Swollow air in stomach while expanding abdomen (Swollow air just like you swollow food).
  • Make kaki mudra then inhale air from mouth and bring neck forward to swollow air and repeat this process.
  • Swollow air 10 times or until stomach is full.
  • Keep control in your neck muscles so air doesn't come out through burp.
  • Store air for 10-30s in beginning.
  • Pass Air through Large Intestine (Can do inverted postures such as Pashinee Mudra).
  • Air should pass through anus easily.
  • After experience store air for 1-2 mins and perform Nauli/Agnisar Kriya.

II. When to do?

  • Anytime, but if you had food then after 3h.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Daily with Surya Namaskar

OR

  • Daily for 1 Month (if problem persist then after it),
  • 3 Times per week for 1 Month (if problem persist then after it),
  • Then move on to 2 Times per week till problem is solved.

IV. Limitations?

  • No limitations.

V. Any other necessary informations?

  • You can sit in any comfortable Asana.
  • You can breathe normally while inhaling air in stomach.
  • It can take time to learn so don't hurry.
  • Not for old age peoples.
  • Don't make it a lifestyle, do only when needed.

1.1.2.1 Danta Mula Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Take cathechu powder (or mix of haldi, namak, sarso tel) and soak it whole night.
  • In morning take sip of that water and massage teeths as well as roots of teeths with it for 5-8 mins.

II. When to do?

III. What are the Repetitions?

1.1.2.2 Jihva Shodhana Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Apply makhkhan on complete tongue.
  • Take first three fingers and rub the tongue (bringing fingers from inside to out) while stretching the tongue completely out as much possible for 2-3 mins.
  • Stretch your tongue using both the hands just like taking milk out of a cow for 8-12 times.
  • Using Tongue Cleaner, clean the tongue completely.

II. When to do?

  • Daily

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Do it Daily.

IV. Any other necessary informations?

  • Nails cutted properly.
  • No problem if there is vomit sensation but try to resist it.
  • Do only empty stomach.

1.1.2.3 Karna Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Take Index/Ring Finger, wet it and press inside ear with some pressure and rotate it for 1-2 mins.
    1. Take Narial/Sarson Tel + 2 pieces of Lahsun and boil it.
    2. Allow it to rest.
    3. Apply 2-3 drops in both the ears.
    4. Massage a little from outside(Front and back of ears).

II. When to do?

  • Part 1 Daily.
  • Part 2 once a week.

III. Any other necessary informations?

  • Wash hands properly brfore performing.
  • Cut nails short.

1.1.3 Hrid Dhauti

1.1.3.1 Vastra Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Take Vastra Dhauti Patti(4 ungli chaudi, 15 hath lambi)
  • Wash it and boil it in with water for 5-8mins.
  • Take another container with warm water add 1-2 pinch salt and keep the patti in it.
  • Sit ukhdu, take another container in front of you with little warm water and fold the patti in circular from front so it can go easily inside mouth.
  • Bring tongue out and keep the patti on tongue and chew the patti little (not much) so that the patti can go easily inside just as food.
  • Keep warm water in a jug to drink (sip by sip) when you are not able to swollow the patti.
  • Repeat this on by taking 2-3 inches patti putting it on tongue and swollowing it.
  • Keep 2-3 feet patti out so to take the swollowed patti out.
  • Go slowly with prctice when you can swollow patti till stomach keep patti for 5 mins in stomach
  • Swollow till stomach and after 5-7 days of tries of swollowing patti in stomach do Agnisar or Nauli Kriya so patti can clean the stomach.
  • Then take out the patti slowly.

II. When to do?

  • Morning empty stomach.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • 1 time per week at starting for 1 month.
  • Then move on to 2 times per month for 1 year.
  • Then 1-2 times per month as necessary.

IV. Limitations?

  • Avoid heavy food for day like in Vaman.

V. Any other necessary informations?

  • Control on coughing or vomiting sensation.
  • Keep patti in touch with tongue.
  • Don't try to swollow whole patti at beginning go with practice.(You will need 12-15 days tries).
  • Then after when you are able to swollow whole patti, practice to keep it inside stomach for 5 mins the after 5-7 times doing it go for Agnisar/Nauli.

1.1.3.3 Vaman Dhauti

I. How to do?

  • Morning Empty stomach, get 2 litres of warm water with sendha namak of 1 tsp in 1L.
  • Sit ukhdu and drink at moderate speed whole 1.5-2L(Drink till larynx or kanth).
  • Stand up, bend forward and by mid 2 fingers press at inside the mouth to vomit out all the water drank.
  • Lay in shavasan for few minutes.
  • When done empty stomach it is known as Kunjal and when done after 3h of food intake it is Vyaghra.

II. When to do?

  • Morning empty stomach for Kunjal.
  • After 3h of food for Vyaghra.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Start with 1 time per week till clean body.
  • 2 times per month can be done for life.

IV. Limitations?

  • No milk products.
  • No heavy food for day.

V. Any other necessary informations?

  • Nails cut short, hands clean.
  • Do Vyaghra only after 3h or more after food when you feel you ate something wrong or food is not digested.
  • Little Blood can come but if more then take rest and do take precautions while performing next time.
  • Can be done anytime in year, just in winter then at home.

1.3 Neti

1.3.1 Jala Neti

I. How to do?

  • Take jala neti tumbler of steel (Beneficial if copper tamba).
  • Keep towel to clean the mucus coming out.
  • Take little warm water(Just as warm as it can be drinked in a go.) and add sendha namak(1 pinch).
  • Sit ukhdu just as you sit in for desi toilet.
  • Bend little front and insert front of tumbler on any one nostril which is open good, let water come out from second nostril.
  • Dry both the nostrils completely take some time (As you can learn from YT).
  • Do again from another nostril.
  • Again dry nostrils.
  • Do Kapalabhati (While standing & front bend) 50-100 times breathing from mouth to nose (75-25 ratio)
  • 2-4 drops of desi ghee or badam tel.

II. When to do?

  • Morning.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Daily till nose stays clean.
  • Then 3 times per week for 1 month.
  • Then 2 times per week for rest of life.

IV. Any other necessary informations?

  • Breathe through mouth not through nose or else water can go inside upper nasal.
  • Dry nostrils completely.
  • Try to do while little warm climate and avoid in rainy season.

1.3.2 Sutra Neti

I. How to do?

  • Take Neti Sutra (Not rubber), wash it, wax coated side with soap and normal water and other side with very warm water and soak it.
  • Boil some water add sendha namak (1-2 pinch)and soak the soft side of sutra in water
  • Sit ukhdu just as neti.
  • Take jala neti tumbler, put warm water and add some salt and dip sutra(soft side) in it 1 time.
  • Twist the whole soft side and bend the wax side little so that it comes out from mouth easily.
  • Take breathe from nose and exhale from mouth during process.
  • Insert sutra from one nostril and keep inserting slowly and by ptting first 2 fingers in mouth grab the sutra and take it out from mouth.
  • Keep rotating while inserting so no problem is faced.
  • Clean the nose and spit if mucus in mouth.
  • Wash sutra as before, first from water then from soap and then soak soft side in the neti tumbler.
  • Do same from other nostril.

II. When to do?

  • Morning before Jala Neti.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Daily till nose stays clean.
  • Then 3 times per week for 1 month.
  • Then 2 times per week for rest of life.

IV. Limitations?

  • Do Jala neti just after sutra neti so if thread particles are left then it can be removed.

V. Any other necessary informations?

  • Blood, tears can come from nose, need not to worry.
  • You can apply sarson tel on sutra before inserting.
  • Apply tel in both nostrils night before you are goiing to perform.
  • Do Jala neti just after sutra neti so if thread particles are left then it can be removed.

1.3.4 Ghrita Neti (Tel Neti)

I. How to do?

  • Take shuddh ghee or sarson tel (Little warm).
  • Lay down.
  • Take breathe from mouth.
  • Put 5-10 drops in both nostrils.
  • Then Inhale very slowly from nose and exhale from mouth 5-10 times.
  • Rub nose while lying few times.

II. When to do?

  • Night, before bed.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Daily at night.

1.5 Trataka

1.5.1 Antaranga Trataka & 1.5.2 Bahiranga Trataka

I. How to do?

  • Sit Comfortably.
  • First Bahiranga Tratak- Start with bindu tratak then go to Deepak Tratak.
  • Then Antaranga Tratak for as long as want (While bringing the image of object you had tratak on).

II. When to do?

  • Daily at night.
  • Bindu trataka for 3 Months then go to Deepak of tel.

III. Limitations?

  • Good Lifestyle.

IV. Any other necessary informations?

  • Don't force yourself much.
  • Start with 1 min and as you improve go max to 15 mins.
  • Start with bindu then go to deepak for Bahiranga.

1.6 Kapalabhati

1.6.1 Vata Karma

Normal Kapalabhati Pranayama.

1.6.2 Vyut Karma

I. How to do?

  • Take a vessel with 2L of warm water and add 1tsp salt.
  • Stand to do the cleansing process and keep the vessel in front of you at a height till your chest.
  • Bend your neck towards the vessel and grab the water from both the hands just as you do to gargle.
  • Inhale that water from nose and it will get stored in mouth.
  • Spit the water out when mouth gets filled with water.
  • Do this till the vessel gets empty or 10-15 times.
  • Dry the nose completely just as you know while using towel.
  • Clean the mucus coming out while drying the nose.

II. When to do?

  • Morning.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Since you are performing Neti you can do 1 time per week till healthy.
  • Then as upon you.

IV. Any other necessary informations?

  • Mix salt completely.
  • Not in Rainy season.
  • 2-3 drops of tel or ghee in both the nostrils.

1.6.3 Sheet Karma

I. How to do?

  • Reverse of Vyut Karma Kapalabhati.
  • Here drink water from mouth and spit it out form nostrils.

II. When to do?

  • Morning.

III. What are the Repetitions?

  • Same as Vyut Karma.

IV. Any other necessary informations?

  • Same as Vyut Karma.
