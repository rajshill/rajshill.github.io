Training Of Body

2. Strengthing (Driddhata) → Asana

3. Steadying (Sthirata) → Mudra

4. Calming (Dhairyata) → Pratyahara

5. Lightness (Laghima) → Pranayama

6. Perception (Pratyakshatwa) → Dhyana

7. Isolation (Nirlipatata) → Samadhi

These all gives Freedom
