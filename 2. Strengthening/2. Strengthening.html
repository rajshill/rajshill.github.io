Sequence(11)

1. Surya Namaskar (16mins)

In First chakra right leg back, second left leg.

  1. Week 1 - 4mins 2 times
  2. Week 2 - 8mins 4 times
  3. Week 3 - 10mins 5 times
  4. Week 4 - 12mins 6 times
  5. Week 5 - 16mins 8 times

2. Shirsasan - 3 times 60s each. (3mins)

Normal breathing, Go as- 1 time 30s, 1 time 45s, 1 time 60s, 2 times 30s, 2 times 60s, 3 times 30s, 3 times 60s.

3. Tadasan --- Tiryak Tadasan --- Padhastasan --- Ardh Chakrasan (10mins)

  1. Tadasan (5mins)- 5 times, 30s then go to 60s
  2. Tiryak Tadasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each.

    Exhale while bending

  3. Padhastasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each.
  4. Ardh Chakrasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each.

    Hands at back either interlocked or fisted, bend back inhaling.

4. Garudasan --- Sarvang Pushti --- Utkatasan (9mins)

  1. Garudasan (3mins) - 3 times, as much possible

    Right leg straight first, right hand elbow front then vice versa, normal breathing, concentrate on one point.

  2. Sarvang Pushti (3mins) - 3 times each side.

    Leg spaced, inhale and hands at top making fist, right in front, exhaling bend at right, interchange fist and bend left and so on.

  3. Utkatasan (Chair pose) (3mins) - 3 times, 60s each.

5. Vrikshasan --- Trikonasan (4.5mins)

  1. Vrikshasan (3mins) - 3 times, as much possible

    Right leg straight first, left till up possible, stretch hand up as possible, normal breathing, concentrate on one point, do with another leg. Body straight.

  2. Trikonasan (1.5mins) - 3 times each side, 15s each .

    Leg spaced, inhale, hands side up, exhale, inhale exhale and bend right, stretch top hand out. do both sides

6. Sarvangasan --- Halasan --- Ushtarasan --- Dhanurasan (5.5mins)

  1. Sarvangasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each.

    Weight on neck back and shoulders, straight body balance by hands and elbows, normal breathing.

  2. Halasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each .

    Normal breathing, bring leg up then back, leg as back as possible, hands control back then straight on floor.

  3. Ushtarasan (1min) - 3 times, 20s each .

    Knees spaced till shoulders, take both hands back right and left for holding feets while inhaling. breathe normally.

  4. Dhanurasan (Boat Pose) (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each .

    Inhale and go up to boat pose, try to see sky.

7. Paschhimotanasan --- Bhujangasan --- Shashankasan --- Chakrasan --- Shashankasan (6mins)

  1. Paschhimotanasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, max 30s each.

    Seated, Hands up from side inhaling, bend forward exhaling.

  2. Bhujangasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each .

    Inhaling go up, elbows straight, legs attached

  3. Shashankasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each .

    Sit on vajrasan, Knees spaced till shoulders and legs fingers attached, take both hands front between knees, inhale and exhale and take hands as front as possible, breathe normally.

  4. Chakrasan (1.5mins) - 3 times, 30s each .

    Lie on back, knees and feet spaced little, do chakrasan while inhaling, then breathe normally, come back exhaling.

8. Baddha Konasan --- Matsyasan --- Ardh Matsyendra Asan (3mins)

  1. Baddha Konasan (Butterfly) (1.5mins) - 25 to 50 times.
  2. Matsyasan - Hold for 30s.

    Lie on back, apply padmasan, head little up front, then touch head top at floor, grab feet, hold, then put elbow below for neck to straight, open padmasan.

  3. Ardh Matsyendra Asan (1min) - 2 times each side, 30s each.

    Left leg down, right leg crossed, right hand rotate from back, left hand touch right foot, take head towards hand at back. breathe normally while hold.

9. Mayurasan (5mins)

10. Agnisar Kriya(Then go to Nauli) (5mins)

11. Shavasan (3mins)
